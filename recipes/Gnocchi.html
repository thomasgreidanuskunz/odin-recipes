Gnocchi recipe

Description

This comforting dish is a classic Italian-inspired meal, packed with flavor and easy to make.

It takes around 20 minutes to prepare and works great as a hearty lunch or dinner.

Ingredients

  1. -500g Gnocchi (store-bought or homemade)
  2. -300g Ground beef (lean or regular, your choice)
  3. -1 can (400g) of crushed tomatoes
  4. -1 small onion, finely chopped
  5. -2 cloves of garlic, minced
  6. -2 tbsp olive oil or butter
  7. -Salt, pepper, and Italian herbs (basil, oregano)
  8. -Parmesan cheese for topping (optional)

How to prepare.

Start by heating the olive oil or butter in a pan over medium heat. Add the chopped onions and garlic, and saut√© until soft. Add the ground beef and cook until browned. Then pour in the crushed tomatoes and season with salt, pepper, and herbs. Let it simmer for 10 minutes. In a separate pot, boil the gnocchi until they float (about 2 to 3 minutes), then drain and combine with the sauce. Mix well, top with Parmesan if desired, and enjoy!
