Pizza recipe

Description

This simple homemade pizza recipe delivers a delicious crispy crust and flavorful toppings with minimal effort.

Perfect for a quick meal, and fully customizable to your taste.

Ingredients

  1. -200g all-purpose flour
  2. -120ml warm water
  3. -1 tsp dry yeast
  4. -1 tbsp olive oil
  5. -1/2 tsp salt
  6. -100g tomato sauce (store-bought or homemade)
  7. -100g shredded mozzarella cheese
  8. -4–5 slices of pepperoni or other toppings of your choice
  9. -Dried oregano and chili flakes (optional)

How to prepare.

In a bowl, mix the warm water, yeast, and a pinch of sugar. Let it sit for 5–10 minutes until foamy. Add flour, salt, and olive oil, then knead into a smooth dough. Let it rise for 30–60 minutes. Roll out the dough into a circle, spread the tomato sauce, sprinkle mozzarella, and add your toppings. Bake at 220°C (430°F) for 10–15 minutes or until the crust is golden and the cheese is bubbly. Slice and enjoy your homemade pizza!
