Anabolic ration pig recipe

Description

This is a simple yet tasty option to help you hit your macros with ease and speed.

It takes less them 15 minutes to prepare, and it can feed a whole family.

Ingredients

  1. -900g Ground pig meat.
  2. -200g Frozen spinach
  3. -200g Sauerkraut
  4. -30g Honey or sugar
  5. -1 Onion
  6. -3 Cloves of garlic
  7. Salt and other seasoning. As much as you would want
  8. -1,5 Cup of water

How to prepare.
